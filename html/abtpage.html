About

What?

I have compiled data on the bullet drop trajectories of the game, Hunt Showdown 1896. There are multiple types of ammunition and the drop is unique to each type. This website serves as a compilation of my data and as a tool for the hunt community to utilize to learn more about the ballistics in the game. Each page will show several recommended holdovers to use at certain distances over the drop range of the ammo type.

Why?

The reason I made this website was because Hunt had received a huge update that added the bullet drop system to the game. This is my favorite game and as someone who has played it for a long time before there was bullet drop it was tricky to adjust to the new system. I thought that I should make this website for new and veteran players alike to help them learn about the ballistics of the game so that they could perform better.

Who?

I am Ivan, a college student with a large fondness for historical firearms and videogames. I discovered,Hunt Showdown 1896, a few years ago and immediately fell in love with the rustic setting and the challenging gameplay. It hit all the right spots when it comes to what I want in a video game. At this point I would say that I am pretty decent at the game, and can generally hold my own against enemy players. I hope the info on this website helps somebody else improve as well. Thanks for checking out my website.
