Welcome to Hunt Bullet Drop

The purpose of this website is to serve as a tool for players of the game, Hunt Showdown 1896. This is a resource for players to use to gain an understanding about the bullet drop mechanics of the game. Click on the ammo above to navigate to the pages on the individual types of ammo and how they drop.

News

There was a recent update to the game which added a new variety of ammo which is subsonic. Due to the change, the data will need to be tested and updated in accordance with this new ammo.
